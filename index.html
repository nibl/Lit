Lit

is an app to teach reading to adults on smartphones and in web browsers. One billion people worldwide cannot read. Technology now gives people the means to learn anywhere, anytime. It's in their pockets.

We need your help

Here are the main tasks. Keep in mind that users cannot read properly. They do not use the Internet or text-based media the way you do. Everything, including support will need to be audio-visual:

  • Create lessons
  • Code javascript (PhoneGap/Cordova for Android)
  • Design an entirely audio-visual UI/UX for people who cannot read
  • Design/Draw expressive images
  • Record voice-overs
  • Find and reach out to potential users
